On-Site & Off-Site

On-Site Events
Host at The Captain's Table
Reserve part or all of our space for your event. Choose from the Afterdeck bar area, our main dining room, the patio, or a full buyout. Our staff handles setup, service, and cleanup — you just show up.
  • Patio Room — minimum 30 guests
  • Dining Room — minimum 45 guests
  • $300 non-refundable deposit to hold your date
  • Choose from 15+ appetizers, 14+ entrées
  • Beer, wine & open bar packages available
  • 3-hour or 2.5-hour event formats
  • Salad, bread, soft drinks & coffee included
Off-Site Catering
We Come to You
Our catering team brings the Captain's Table experience directly to your venue. Perfect for backyard parties, corporate offices, venues without a kitchen, and community events throughout Orange County and the Hudson Valley.
  • Delivery & setup available for additional charge
  • Hot & cold food transport
  • Paper products available upon request
  • Gluten-sensitive & vegan options available
  • Select items available with one week's notice
  • 3% discount for cash payment
  • Call (845) 783-0209 ext. 2 to reserve
