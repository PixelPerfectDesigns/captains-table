The Afterdeck
Our Story

Over 90 years of good food and good people.

What started as a roadside rest stop along old Route 17 has grown into one of Orange County's most beloved gathering places. The Hafenecker family has poured generations of care into every corner — from the warm wood interior to the 1/3-acre sand beach out back.

We welcome everyone. Young singles, date nights, families with kids in the sandbox, retirees, and everyone in between. The menu is broad, the portions are generous, and the welcome is always genuine.
